Green Monkeys

The morning-after accountability app.

For anyone who drinks more than they meant to and spends the next morning with the Green Monkeys on one shoulder and Captain Paranoia on the other. This app puts them both to work for you.

The Plan

Before a night out, sober-you records a 30-second video and a list of promises: max drinks, water between rounds, leave by eleven, no karaoke. Your promises, your list — it learns the ones you invent.

🐒

The Session

At the times you chose, the Green Monkeys check in on your phone and Watch with the plan you made — and one tap opens the camera so drunk-you can leave a message for tomorrow. Tomorrow-you will wince.

🫣

The Morning After

Captain Paranoia runs the debrief: watch the evidence, judge each promise, confess the booze crimes, and score yourself 0–5 — from "behaved like a normal person" to "can't show my face again".

📉

The Pattern

Your idiot rate, your average score, the promises you keep breaking and the crimes you keep committing. Make the same promise again and the Monkeys will quote your record back at you.

📱

The Widget

The counter sits on your home screen and Lock Screen, ticking over at midnight. Green when you're behaving. Red when you're rebuilding.

🔒

Private by design

No account. No cloud. No analytics. Your videos are stored encrypted on your phone, excluded from backups, locked behind Face ID — and there is deliberately no share button. Read the privacy policy.

A straight word: Green Monkeys is a self-awareness tool for social drinkers, built around dark humour and honest mornings. It is not a treatment for alcohol dependence. If drinking is genuinely worrying you, start with the NHS's judgement-free guidance at nhs.uk/live-well/alcohol-advice.
